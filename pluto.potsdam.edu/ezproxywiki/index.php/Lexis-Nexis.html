Sample configuration

 T Lexis/Nexis
 U http://www.lexisnexis.com/universe
 U http://www.lexisnexis.com.au/cui/uni-xxxxxxxx
 U http://www.lexisnexis.com.au/cui/uni-login/default.htm?login.asp?uni=xxxxxxxx
 HJ web.lexis-nexis.com
 HJ www.lexis-nexis.com
 DJ lexis-nexis.com
 DJ lexis-nexis.com.au
 DJ lexis.com
 DJ lexisnexis.com
 DJ lexisnexis.com.au
 DJ nexis.com
 DJ cispubs.com
 DJ www.nexis.com
 DJ www.lexis.com
 DJ www.lexis-nexis.com
 DJ www.lexis-nexis.newsalert.com
 DJ www.lexisnexis.com
 DJ www.lexisnexis.com.au
 DJ web.nexis.com
 DJ web.lexis.com
 DJ web.lexis-nexis.com
 DJ web.lexis-nexis.newsalert.com
 DJ web.lexisnexis.com
 DJ web.lexisnexis.com.au
 Find GetCookie("LNAUTH")
 Replace "LNAUTH-IP"
 Find NAME="_PRIORREFERER" VALUE="http://
 Replace NAME="_PRIORREFERER" VALUE="http://^A

Alternate configuration

This simpler config, dated 2006-03-04, appears on this Useful Utilities page and may be suitable for LexisNexis users who do not need the Australian domains included above. 

T LexisNexis
U http://web.lexis-nexis.com/
DJ lexis-nexis.com
DJ lexisnexis.com
DJ lexis.com
DJ cispubs.com
HJ web.lexis-nexis.com
HJ web.lexisnexis.com
HJ www.lexis-nexis.com
HJ www.lexisnexis.com
Find GetCookie("LNAUTH")
Replace "LNAUTH-IP"
Find NAME="_PRIORREFERER" VALUE="http://
Replace NAME="_PRIORREFERER" VALUE="http://^A

Requirements

Both of the above configurations require EZproxy 2.0j GA (2003-06-02) or later.
