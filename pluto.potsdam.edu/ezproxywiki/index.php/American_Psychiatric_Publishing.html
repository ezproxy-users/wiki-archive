Sample configuration

This config, dated 2006-06-08, appears on this Useful Utilities page. 

T Journals of American Psychiatric Publishing
U http://www.psychiatryonline.org/
DJ psychiatryonline.org

RSS feed support

To offer support for American Psychiatric Publishing's RSS feeds, enclose the config stanza between the following two lines: 

AnonymousURL -RE +http://[a-z]+\.psychiatryonline\.org/rss/[a-z]+\.xml
...
AnonymousURL -*

For example: 

AnonymousURL -RE +http://[a-z]+\.psychiatryonline\.org/rss/[a-z]+\.xml
T Journals of American Psychiatric Publishing
U http://www.psychiatryonline.org/
DJ psychiatryonline.org
AnonymousURL -*

Users could then subscribe to the proxied feed for American Journal of Psychiatry at http://ajp.psychiatryonline.org.ezproxy.yourlib.org/rss/current.xml (substitute your EZProxy server domain for ezproxy.yourlib.org). Other journal feeds could be accessed by changing "ajp" to the appropriate acronym.

Requirements

The configuration for RSS feed support requires EZproxy 4.0a GA (2006-08-02) or later.
